How to Create a Post in eMedia Library

  1. Navigate to the Posts Tab
    Click on the Posts tab in the main menu to access the post management area.
  2. Create a New Post
    Select the Create Post button to start a new post.
  3. Fill Out the Details
    • Name: Enter the title of your post.
    • Description: Provide a brief description.
    • Date: Add the relevant date.
    • Tags and Category: Assign tags and a category if needed for better organization.
  4. Save Your Post
    Once all the details are complete, click Save to publish your post.
